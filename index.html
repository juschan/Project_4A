Synopsis

This project prototype aims to present a 'dashboard'' to the user based on their current insurance coverage across multiple products and providers. It also aims to present planning and documentation 'tools' to encourage planning for future events including death (ie. setting up of will), medical decisions (ie. advanced medical directive) and opportunities to buy products (ie. link to direct purchase insurance website).


Background

Most Singaporeans have, as part of their citizenship and/or national service obligations, some forms of insurance coverage. These may include:

  • Life and personal accident coverage for Fulltime National Service Men, NSmen etc;
  • Health insurance via Medishield Life for Singaporean citizens and Permanent Residents;
  • Disability insurance via Eldershield.

This is in addition to those provided by their employers or those bought individually.
